Tech Path AI is an AI-powered career guidance and assessment platform designed to help Computer Science (CS) and Software Engineering (SE) students make informed career decisions. The system evaluates students through structured assessments in **personality, problem-solving, critical thinking, and creative thinking**, and provides personalized career recommendations, guidance, and insights. The platform aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world career paths by offering skill tracking, leaderboard features, and information about various tech fields through roadmaps and career descriptions.

Live Demo: [https://tech-path-ai.vercel.app](https://tech-path-ai.vercel.app)

## ğŸ›‘ Problem Statement

Many CS/SE students struggle to identify suitable career paths due to lack of guidance, unstructured learning, and limited exposure to different technology domains. Traditional career counseling methods are often generic and do not provide actionable insights based on individual skills and thinking patterns. Tech Path AI addresses these issues by providing AI-powered assessments, personalized career guidance, structured learning modules, and gamified motivation to help students make informed career decisions and track their progress.

## ğŸš€ Key Features

### 1. User Authentication & Authorization

* User registration and login
* Email verification
* Password reset functionality
* Role-based access (Admin / User)

### 2. Career Assessment System

The assessment module is divided into multiple categories:

#### a) Personality Assessment

* Evaluates behavioral traits and personal preferences
* Helps understand the userâ€™s working style and interests

#### b) Problem-Solving Assessment

* Measures logical thinking and analytical abilities
* Suitable for evaluating foundational CS/SE skills

#### c) Critical Thinking Assessment

* This assessment is further divided into four subcategories:

1. Management & Planning
2. Leadership & Organization
3. Interest in People vs. Systems
4. Communication & Teamwork

#### d) Creative Thinking Assessment

* Assesses creativity, innovation, and idea-generation skills

### 3. AI-Powered Career Recommendations

* Provides personalized career suggestions based on assessment results
* Helps students explore and understand different career paths in the tech industry

### 4. Career Exploration & Learning Resources

* Students can explore various career options in different technology domains
* Provides structured roadmaps for multiple tech fields
* Displays upcoming technology-related events
* Allows students to read educational and career-focused blogs

### 5. User Dashboard & Analytics

* A personalized dashboard to view overall progress and statistics
* Displays assessment results, skill growth, and learning progress

### 6. Achievements & Gamification

* An achievements section containing:

1. Leaderboard rankings
2. Earned badges
3. XP (experience points)

* Encourages motivation and consistent learning through gamification

### 7. Skill Tracking & Learning Assistant (LUMO)

* Integrated AI learning assistant LUMO guides users throughout their learning journey
* Users can enroll in available technology domains
* After enrollment, LUMO assigns structured learning modules
* Each module includes:

1. Multiple lessons
2. Learning content and resource links
3. Lesson completion tracking

* Users can:

1. Mark lessons as completed
2. Earn XP points after completing lessons and modules
3. View earned XP on the achievements page

### 8. Quiz System

* Each learning module contains three quizzes
* One quiz is unlocked after the completion of 10 lessons
* Quizzes help reinforce learning and assess understanding
* Users earn additional XP points upon quiz completion

### 9. Settings & Profile Management

* Users can edit their profile information
* Change account passwords securely

### 10. Admin Panel

* Manage questions and assessments
* View user statistics and results
* Control platform content
